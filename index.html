Our story

Built on a single idea — service with a passion.

What began in 1991 with a single warehouse for Hindustan Unilever in coastal Andhra has grown — year by year, customer by customer — into one of South India's most trusted logistics partners.

Today, we operate a multi-state warehousing network across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka, partnering with HUL, ITC, Castrol, Pidilite, Marico and KCIL.

Our success is measured less in square footage and tonnage, and more in the trust our clients place in us — some, like HUL, partnering with us for over three decades.

SN
Sridhar Nuthakki
Founder & Managing Partner
